Spending time away from the world brings a calm contentment. It delivers peace and happiness in vast volumes, but sparse substance. In the shadows we can limit our concerns, but the problems still await us in the light.

The world has many problems, but when we isolate ourselves we see only our part of the puzzle. It is by coming together that we may find the pieces to construct the whole picture.

*Nex.RIP* is a means to bring me back into the world, and to help others in sharing themselves.

We are all musicians, and we have always been. We want to share music with the world, and to help others follow the same path. I want to rejoin that community of dreams; the one that has given me so much and kept me alive all these years.

If you want to hear my music, or know the music of others, then [Choons](/choons) should serve you well.

The other path is concerned with less ethereal realms: resolving hopes toward realities. If you want to explore the thoughts behind the dreams or learn to dream yourself, [Secrets](/secrets) is the line to follow.

I have been in the shadows for far too long. It is time to rejoin the light.
